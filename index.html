Simple Process

How Valuna works

Every marketplace order follows a protected route designed to keep buyers, sellers and delivery information secure.

01
List or Browse
Sellers list cards instantly with no approval wait. Buyers browse verified listings filtered by game, grade and condition.
02
Offer & Pay
Buyer pays through protected checkout. The seller receives order confirmation and Valuna intake instructions, not the buyer's private delivery address.
03
Valuna Verifies
Seller ships to the Valuna Newcastle hub. Valuna checks the card, confirms the verification route, then dispatches to the buyer.
04
Done
Buyer receives the card after Valuna dispatch. The order moves through completion and seller feedback is tied to reliability and verification outcomes.
